About

Dear Visitor 👋,



My name is Alex Smith, and I’m a web developer 💻 and designer 🎨 who’s passionate about creating beautiful, functional websites. Over the past few years, I’ve worked on a variety of projects — from small business sites to full-scale web apps — always with a focus on clean code, responsive design, and user-friendly experiences ✨.


What began as a curiosity quickly turned into a career I truly enjoy. I love the process of turning ideas 💡 into interactive, real-world solutions that not only look great but also make people’s lives a bit easier.


I primarily work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React ⚛️, but I'm always learning and exploring new tools and technologies. Tech is always evolving, and I enjoy evolving with it! 🔄


When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me sketching UI concepts ✏️, reading about design trends 📚, or relaxing with a good coffee ☕ and a walk around the city 🌆.


Thanks for visiting my portfolio — I hope you enjoy exploring my work. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to chat or collaborate! 🤝


Warmly,
Alex 🚀
